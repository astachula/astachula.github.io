Anthony Stachula

My second hobby is Hunting. I have been hunting for 40 years with my best friend from Jr high school. As the years pass we still manage to head to the northern woods and spend time. We mostly hunt white tail deer. But I'll be honest I have gotten to point where standing on 20 by 36 inch platform is getting old especially in 10 degree weather. It's not so much the hunting anymore it's more just getting together and spending good times with each other. But that's kind of the whole experience of hunting.

HMy favorite part of hunting is walking in the woods smelling the pines and feeling the fresh northern woods air, and of course the thrill of that big buck coming down the tree line and taking home meat for the year.
